Help & Information

Why do we need this information?

We collect your personal information to verify your identity and ensure account security. This helps prevent fraud and protects both you and other users.

Document Requirements

  • Document must be current and valid
  • All text should be clearly visible
  • No glare or shadows on the document
  • Document should fill most of the frame
  • Accepted formats: JPG, PNG (max 5MB)

Face Verification Tips

  • Ensure good lighting on your face
  • Look directly at the camera
  • Remove sunglasses or hat
  • Keep a neutral expression
  • Make sure your face fits within the guide circle

Data Security

Your information is encrypted and stored securely. We follow industry-standard security practices to protect your personal data and will never share it without your consent.
